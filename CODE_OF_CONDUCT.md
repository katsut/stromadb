# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in the StromaDB project a harassment-free experience for everyone,
regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and the community.

Unacceptable behavior includes: harassment, insulting or derogatory comments, personal or political
attacks, publishing others' private information without permission, and other conduct that would
reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and when
an individual is representing the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported privately to the maintainers
via GitHub's private vulnerability/abuse reporting on this repository. Maintainers will review and
respond as they deem appropriate and will respect the privacy and security of the reporter.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
